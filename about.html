About Medsera

Our Mission

Medsera was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, accurate, and accessible medicine information to everyone in India. We believe that empowered patients make better health decisions, and we're committed to breaking down medical jargon into understandable language.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of healthcare professionals and technology experts, Medsera emerged from a shared frustration with the lack of reliable, India-specific medicine information online. Our platform has grown to become one of the most trusted sources for medicine information in India, serving millions of users each month.

What We Do

Medsera provides comprehensive information about:

  • Indian brand and generic medicines
  • Medicine uses, side effects, and interactions
  • Normal ranges for medical tests
  • Health conditions and treatment options
  • Latest medical research and news
