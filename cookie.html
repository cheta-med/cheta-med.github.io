What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, such as remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and analyzing how you interact with a site. Cookies do not harm your device and do not give us access to your personal files or data without your consent.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They allow you to navigate our site and use its features, such as accessing secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This information allows us to improve site performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences and settings, such as language or region, so you don’t need to reconfigure them each time you visit.
  • Advertising Cookies: We may use cookies to deliver relevant advertisements to you and to measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns. These may come from third-party providers like Google AdSense.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties, such as analytics providers, advertisers, or embedded content providers (e.g., YouTube, social media widgets). These third parties may collect data about your browsing behavior across multiple websites. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy and cookie policies of these third parties.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us enhance your browsing experience in several ways. They allow us to personalize content, remember login details, track website performance, and deliver advertisements that are relevant to your interests. Without cookies, certain features of our website may not work as intended.

Managing and Disabling Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these options in your browser’s "Settings" or "Preferences" menu. You may choose to block or delete cookies at any time, but please note that some parts of our website may not function properly if you disable essential cookies.
